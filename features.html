Core Features

PATENT-PENDING

BuildingBlocks Technology

Patent-pending rapid project creation system using pre-built templates loaded with activities, resources, materials, drawings, and risk data. Results in 90% reduction of project creation time including CPM schedule, budgeting, and baseline approval

REVOLUTIONARY

Dynamic Scheduling

Non-deterministic scheduling that automatically adapts to daily changes and resource availability. Unlike traditional systems, recognizes that scheduling activities is worthless when resources aren't available. Results in huge cost savings through optimal resource allocation with no idle workers

Complete Project Control

Integrated oversight of all project aspects: schedule, cost, safety, quality, and resources in one system. This AI-driven process reduces Project Control headcount and enables managers to use less expensive resources, reducing operational costs

Cost Estimation

Accurate project estimation with BuildingBlocks technology for 10x faster creation and higher accuracy than traditional methods. Perfect alignment of estimate to Cost Code makes cost tracking easy

Budgeting & Cost Control

Real-time budget management with automatic variance tracking, forecasting, and granular activity-level cost control. Activity Based Cost Management results in better control and cost reduction

Health & Safety

Integrated HSE management with compliance tracking, incident reporting, and safety protocol enforcement. Enables managers and inspectors to proactively control and ensure safer work environments

Quality Control

Comprehensive quality management with inspection schedules, photo documentation, and defect tracking. Enables managers and inspectors to proactively control and ensure high quality work while reducing/eliminating cost of rework

Earned Value Management

Performance measurement combining schedule and cost data to predict project outcomes and identify problems early. Helps managers monitor and control for on-time and budget project completion safely

Dynamic Real-time Dashboard

Live project health monitoring with instant updates across all users and project data. Real-time access to project and enterprise data enables executives and managers to control outcomes
